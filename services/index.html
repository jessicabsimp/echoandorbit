// Add-On Service

Meet InstaClaw.bot

Every Echo & Orbit site can be connected to InstaClaw.bot — an AI assistant that gives you the ability to manage your own site through a simple chat interface. Update copy, swap images, change hours, connect your domain — no developer required.

Hosting and maintenance are bundled into your InstaClaw.bot monthly plan, so you get one flat fee that covers everything.

⚠  InstaClaw.bot is designed for content updates and basic site management. Requests that go beyond its scope — or that result in site-breaking changes — may incur a service charge to diagnose and restore.
