// 01 — The Origin

Where It Started

Founded: 2026
Based: Knoxville, TN
Focus: Civic + Nonprofit Tech

Echo & Orbit started with a specific frustration: Tennessee advocates working on early childhood policy had no real digital tools to do their jobs. Tracking legislation, understanding where candidates stood, connecting communities to the process — it was all manual, scattered, and exhausting.

So I built something. A few personal projects to help advocates better track early childhood legislation in Tennessee. Nothing fancy at first — just tools that made real work easier. But the more those tools took shape, the more a bigger pattern became clear.

This wasn't a niche problem. Campaigns, nonprofits, and advocacy organizations across the state — and across the country — were dealing with the same gap. The infrastructure to do meaningful digital work existed. But it was expensive, inaccessible, and built for the elite. The organizations that needed it most were exactly the ones who couldn't afford it.

That's the gap Echo & Orbit was built to close. Not just for early childhood advocates, but for any mission-driven organization that deserves the same digital firepower as the institutions they're trying to hold accountable.

If your work is for the good of others, your tools should be too.

// 02 — What We Stand For

Mission & Values

// Value 01

Radical Accessibility

High-level technology shouldn't be a luxury item. We're dedicated to tearing down the digital gatekeeping that keeps innovative tools in the hands of the elite. Low-Carbon Innovation

We refuse to sacrifice the future to build the present. Innovation shouldn't come at the cost of the environment. Every tool and platform we deploy is built with sustainability in mind — because impact work shouldn't create environmental debt. Read our Climate Pledge →

// Value 03

Rooted Growth

While we reach for the "Orbit," we stay grounded in our "Echo." We prioritize the health of our local Knoxville and Tennessee communities, supporting the businesses and residents who make our home what it is. We grow only as our community grows.

We believe in tech that serves humans, not the other way around. We are open about our use of AI and automation, using these tools to give time back to our clients so they can continue fighting the good fight. Our tech is smart, but our intentions are always human-first.

// 03 — Where We're From

Knoxville Built. Tennessee Rooted.

Echo & Orbit is a Knoxville, Tennessee company — and that's not incidental. The problems we're solving started here. The communities we care most about are here. And the standard we hold ourselves to is shaped by what it means to build something that actually serves the place you call home.
